01 The Project

A retreat between
the falls and the tea.

Lavers Hill is a 20-acre plotted community of forty-four villa plots, gently terraced into the Kotagiri escarpment near Aravenu. Mornings begin with mist threading through the tea, and end with cloud spilling over the cliff edge at sunset.

“Each home must blend seamlessly into the misty hillsides — elegance without disturbing ecological harmony.”

Plots are sized between 22 and 215 cents, served by 20–25 ft internal roads, with two community gardens, underground utilities and a single secured entry. You buy the land — we build, on your terms, when you are ready, or not at all.

02 Why Kotagiri

A hill town with
no queue.

Where Ooty has traffic, Kodai has crowds, and Coonoor is filling up fast — Kotagiri remains what a hill station was meant to be. Quiet roads, unhurried mornings, and skies you can actually see. No honking ghat-road convoys, no plastic markets, no weekend chaos. Just tea, mist, and the occasional langur.

  • SereneNo tourist circuit. No hawker strips. Population a tenth of Ooty.

09 FAQ

Questions,
answered.

How is the title held? Is the land patta?
All plots are sold on individual patta titles, registered in the buyer's name. Survey numbers 23/1A1A & 4B, Jacknarrai Village, Kotagiri Taluk. Layout approved 30 October 2025. Encumbrance certificate and parent documents are shared on the first site visit.
What does the price include?
Plot cost includes the land at the rate per cent, stamp duty & registration assistance, internal road network, underground utilities (power, water, fibre), boundary wall, two community gardens and gated entry. Construction is optional and quoted separately if you choose MGS to build.
Do I have to build, and within how long?
No. There is no compulsory build clause — you can hold the plot indefinitely. If you do build, MGS Builders can manage it end-to-end with a 12–18 month timeline. We can also recommend other Nilgiris-based architects if you'd prefer to bring your own team.
Is this a holiday home or can I live full-time?
Both. The project is designed primarily for weekend and holiday use, but several buyers are full-time residents. Kotagiri has good schools and hospitals; the climate suits year-round living for most.
How does maintenance work after I buy?
A Resident Welfare Association is formed once 60% of plots are sold. Until then, MGS administers a monthly maintenance fee covering security, landscaping, road upkeep and the clubhouse. Concierge services are charged separately, on use.
What about water and power?
A community-wide rainwater harvesting system, two tested borewells and a TWAD water connection feed an underground reservoir. Power is from TANGEDCO with a backup at the clubhouse; every plot has provision for a private inverter or solar setup.
